Why Choose Us

Huge School Campus

The diversification and expansion of the school through adoption of new and topically relevant courses has led to further enhancement of our infastructure in order to fulfill our growing needs

Computer Education

To help students prepare for a wide variety of possible careers and joining classes based on computer education, teaching them how to use computers is very important. The basic knowledge helps them to join advanced courses based on the computer in the future

Music & Dance

Children learn new things, like sounds, words and patterns through music. And with dance, they can explore and control their body movements. These activities are great for development and stimulate connections in the growing brain.

Science Laboratory

The school has three well equipped labs for Biology, Chemistry and Physics. Each Lab allows children to learn actively and engage them in hands on activities.
